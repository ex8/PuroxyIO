Privacy
Clients' privacy is very important to Puroxy. We are committed to safeguarding the information Clients entrust to Puroxy.

The Information We Collect
Puroxy collects personally identifiable information from Clients through online forms for ordering products and services. We may also collect information about how Clients use our website, for example, by tracking the number of unique views received by the pages of the Web site or the domains from which Clients originate. We use "cookies" to track how Clients use our website.

At no time will Puroxy's database of Clients ever be sold to any entity for the purpose of marketing or mailing lists. Personal information will not be sold or otherwise transferred to our business partners without your prior consent, except that we will disclose the information we collect to third parties when, in our good faith judgment, we are obligated to do so under applicable laws.
